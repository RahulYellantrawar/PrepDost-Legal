1. Information We Collect

A. Personal Data

While using our App, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you ("Personal Data"). Personally identifiable information may include, but is not limited to:

  • Email address: If you choose to register or log in using your email.
  • Name: If provided during registration or profile setup.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you access and use the App.
  • Device Data: Information about the mobile device you use to access the App (e.g., device type, operating system, unique device identifiers).

B. Usage Data

When you access the App by or through a mobile device, we may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, the type of mobile device you use, your mobile device unique ID, the IP address of your mobile device, your mobile operating system, the type of mobile Internet browser you use, unique device identifiers and other diagnostic data ("Usage Data").

C. Data from Mock Tests, Study Planner, and AI Chat

  • Mock Test Performance: We collect data related to your performance in mock tests, including scores, time taken, correct answers, incorrect answers, and skipped questions. This data is used to provide you with progress tracking and personalized insights.
  • Study Planner Data: Information you input into the study planner, such as plan titles, topics, start/end dates, daily goals, and topic completion status, is collected to enable the planner's functionality and track your study progress.
  • AI Chat Interactions: We collect logs of your interactions with the AI chat feature (e.g., questions asked, AI responses) to improve the AI's performance, understand user queries, and enhance the service. Your chat data may be used anonymously for analysis and model training.
